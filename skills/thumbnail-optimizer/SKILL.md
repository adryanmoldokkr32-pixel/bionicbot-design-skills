# Thumbnail Optimizer

This skill focuses on creating eye-catching thumbnails that drive high click-through rates (CTR) on social platforms.

## Instructions

1. Identify the core hook or curiosity gap of the content.
2. Use high-contrast colors and large, readable text.
3. Incorporate expressive faces or key product visuals.
4. Ensure clarity at small sizes (mobile view).
5. Test 2-3 variants of text overlay for maximum impact.

## Examples

- "Generate a high-CTR thumbnail for a video titled 'How I built a SaaS in 24 hours'."
- "Create a blog thumbnail for a post about 'Future of AI'."
