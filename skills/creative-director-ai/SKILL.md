# Creative Director AI

This skill acts as a high-level artistic supervisor, ensuring that AI-generated images meet professional aesthetic standards.

## Instructions

1. Review the composition and lighting of generated assets.
2. Suggest prompt modifications to fix common AI artifacts (e.g., hands, distorted text).
3. Enhance the mood using specific artistic styles (e.g., Cinematic, Cyberpunk, Oil Painting).
4. Ensure visual consistency across a series of images.
5. Provide detailed feedback for iterative improvement.

## Examples

- "Critique this AI-generated character and suggest 3 ways to make it more professional."
- "Refine this prompt to get a more cinematic lighting effect."
